## Biography

I am a **Radiologist** with extensive specialized training in both **Pediatric Radiology** and **Musculoskeletal (MSK) Radiology**. My professional journey includes clinical fellowships at leading medical institutions in Thailand and international research experience in Japan and the United States.

My clinical interest focuses on advanced diagnostic imaging, aiming to integrate global research insights into professional practice to provide the highest standard of patient care.
